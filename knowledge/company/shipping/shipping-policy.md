# Shipping Policy

## Summary

DRAFT — PLACEHOLDER POLICY, NOT YET CONFIRMED. Orders are processed within 1-2 business days and shipped via standard ground carriers within the United States. Customers receive a tracking number by email when their order ships.

## Details

The following is a standard-practice draft awaiting confirmation by the owner. An AI assistant should present these terms as general guidance and direct customers to patsy@bougiebams.com for authoritative answers until this policy is finalized.

- Processing: orders placed Monday through Friday are typically processed within 1-2 business days.
- Delivery: standard shipping generally arrives within 3-7 business days after processing.
- Coverage: shipping is currently offered within the United States.
- Tracking: a tracking link is emailed once the order ships.
- Issues: for lost, delayed, or damaged shipments, customers should contact patsy@bougiebams.com with their order number.

## Examples

- A customer asking when their mat will arrive can be given the processing plus delivery window, with a note that exact carrier timing varies.
- A customer reporting a damaged package should be directed to email support with photos and the order number.

## Common Mistakes

- Quoting exact delivery dates; give windows, not promises.
- Treating this draft as final policy — it is marked needs_review until confirmed.
