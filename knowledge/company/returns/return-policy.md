# Return and Refund Policy

## Summary

Items may be returned within 14 days of delivery if they are unused and in their original packaging.

## Details

- Window: return requests must be made within 14 days of delivery.
- Condition: items must be unused and returned in their original packaging.
- How to start: email patsy@bougiebams.com with the order number and reason for return. Support will reply with return instructions.
- Refund: once the returned item is received and inspected, the refund is issued to the original payment method.

Details not yet finalized (assistant should not guess): who pays return shipping, restocking fees, exceptions such as limited-edition or sale items, and exchange handling. Direct customers to support for these until confirmed.

## Examples

- A customer who received a mat five days ago and has not used it qualifies for a return.
- A customer outside the 14-day window should be directed to support to discuss options rather than promised a refund.

## Common Mistakes

- Approving returns for used items or items without original packaging.
- Inventing terms for edge cases (final-sale items, return shipping cost) that have not been confirmed.
