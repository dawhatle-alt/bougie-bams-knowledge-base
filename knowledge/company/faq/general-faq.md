# General Company FAQ

## Summary

Quick answers about Bougie Bams: what it is, where it operates, its newsletter, social channels, and wholesale inquiries.

## Details

What is Bougie Bams? A luxury, intimate mahjong community offering curated mahjong mats, sets, and accessories plus styled live events. Founded by Patsy Miller.

Where is Bougie Bams located? Texas — events run in the north Austin metro area (Leander and Cedar Park area venues).

Is there a newsletter? Yes — the Bougie Circle. Subscribers get access to new collections, upcoming events, limited edition drops, and Patsy's picks. New email subscribers receive a 15% discount offer.

Where can I follow Bougie Bams? Instagram and Pinterest, both at @bougiebams.

Do you offer wholesale? Wholesale inquiries can be submitted through the contact form on bougiebams.com or by emailing patsy@bougiebams.com.

Do I need to know how to play mahjong? No. Events and the community welcome complete beginners through seasoned players.

## Examples

- A visitor asking about a discount can be pointed to the Bougie Circle newsletter signup for 15% off.
- A boutique interested in carrying products should be routed to the wholesale contact option.

## Common Mistakes

- Promising discount stacking or terms beyond the advertised signup offer.
- Assuming events happen outside the Austin area without checking current listings.
