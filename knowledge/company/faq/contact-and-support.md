# Contact and Support

## Summary

Customers can reach the Bougie Bams concierge team by email at patsy@bougiebams.com or through the contact form at bougiebams.com/contact. Support hours are Monday-Friday, 9am-5pm EST.

## Details

Contact channels:

- Email: patsy@bougiebams.com
- Contact form: bougiebams.com/contact, with topics for Order Inquiry, Product Question, Wholesale, Press and Media, and Other
- Social: @bougiebams on Instagram and Pinterest

Hours: Monday through Friday, 9am to 5pm EST. Messages received outside these hours are answered the next business day.

When contacting about an order, customers should include their order number and the email used at checkout so support can locate the order quickly.

## Examples

- An order status question should go to email or the contact form with the Order Inquiry topic.
- A journalist should use the Press and Media topic on the contact form.

## Common Mistakes

- Directing customers to phone support; email and the contact form are the supported channels.
- Forgetting to ask for the order number, which delays resolution.
