# The Bougie Bams Story

## Summary

Bougie Bams is a luxury, intimate mahjong community founded by Patsy Miller. It combines a curated web store of premium mahjong mats, sets, and accessories with beautifully styled live events in the north Austin, Texas area.

## Details

Bougie Bams was born from a love of mahjong and a belief that every gathering deserves to be memorable — stunning, warm, and nothing short of luxurious. The brand rejects plain folding tables and mismatched tiles in favor of intentionally styled tables where every detail, from the linens to the lighting, is chosen with care.

Founder and CEO Patsy Miller describes Bougie Bams as an extension of who she is: a colorful, slightly over-the-top Texan who believes life is better when people gather around a beautiful table.

The business has three connected parts: a web store of curated mahjong mats, sets, and accessories; live ticketed mahjong socials at local venues; and a welcoming community that spans complete beginners to seasoned players.

## Examples

- A customer asking who is behind Bougie Bams can be told about Patsy Miller and the Texas-born, luxury-community positioning.
- An assistant describing the brand should emphasize experience and community, not just products.

## Common Mistakes

- Describing Bougie Bams as only a store — events and community are core to the brand.
- Assuming events are for experienced players only; all levels are welcome.
