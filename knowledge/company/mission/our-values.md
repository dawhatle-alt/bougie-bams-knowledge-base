# Our Values and Standards

## Summary

Bougie Bams stands on three product values — premium materials, artisan craftsmanship, and modern aesthetic — plus a community value: every level of player deserves a seat at a beautiful table.

## Details

Premium materials: Bougie Bams sources high-grade acrylics and metals so each tile has the right weight, sound, and hand-feel.

Artisan craftsmanship: sets are hand-finished with attention to detail, from crisp engraving to flawless polishing.

Modern aesthetic: the brand honors the tradition of the game while reimagining its look for the modern, design-conscious player.

Community: the atmosphere at the table matters as much as the tiles in a player's hand. Events and products are designed so beginners and seasoned players feel equally welcome.

## Examples

- When a customer asks why Bougie Bams products cost more than mass-market sets, point to materials, hand-finishing, and design.
- When describing an event, mention that the styling standard applies to every table, not just featured ones.

## Common Mistakes

- Reducing the brand to aesthetics only; material quality and feel are equal pillars.
- Overpromising specifics not in the catalog — check current product listings before making claims.
