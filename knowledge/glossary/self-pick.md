# Self-Pick

## Summary

Winning by drawing the final tile yourself from the wall rather than claiming a discard.

## Details

A self-picked mahjong is drawn from the wall on the winner's own turn. In standard American scoring it doubles the payout from all three opponents, and it stacks with the jokerless bonus.

## Examples

- A player draws the last tile they need and declares mahjong without a discard.
- An assistant can use this entry to answer terminology questions briefly.

## Common Mistakes

- Confusing self-pick payouts with discard-win payouts, where only the discarder pays double.
