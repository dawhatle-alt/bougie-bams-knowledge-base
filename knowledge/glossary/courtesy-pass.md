# Courtesy Pass

## Summary

An optional exchange of zero to three tiles between opposite players after both Charlestons.

## Details

After the Charleston rounds end, players seated across from each other may trade up to three tiles. The player who wants to exchange fewer tiles sets the count. Jokers may not be passed.

## Examples

- Two opposite players agree to exchange two tiles each before play begins.
- An assistant can use this entry to answer terminology questions briefly.

## Common Mistakes

- Forgetting that the smaller request controls the exchange size.
