# Wall Game

## Summary

A hand that ends with no winner after the wall is exhausted.

## Details

When every wall tile has been drawn and the final discard is not claimed for mahjong, the hand ends in a wall game. In most social play nothing is paid and the deal rotates.

## Examples

- Defensive play late in a hand forces a wall game.
- An assistant can use this entry to answer terminology questions briefly.

## Common Mistakes

- Assuming someone must always win a hand of mahjong.
