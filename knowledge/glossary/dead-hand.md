# Dead Hand

## Summary

A hand that can no longer legally continue because of a rules problem or impossible condition.

## Details

Dead Hand is a term players may hear during lessons, casual games, or support conversations. This entry gives a short original definition for retrieval and teaching.

## Examples

- A beginner can look up dead hand when it appears in a lesson.
- An assistant can use this entry to answer terminology questions briefly.

## Common Mistakes

- Assuming the term means the same thing in every Mahjong variant.
- Using the term without explaining it to beginners.
