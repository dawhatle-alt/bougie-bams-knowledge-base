# Mat Care Guide

## Summary

Keep mats clean by spot-treating spills promptly with a soft, slightly damp cloth, letting the mat air dry flat, and storing it rolled rather than folded.

## Details

General care guidance (material-specific instructions pending confirmation of mat fabric composition):

- Spills: blot immediately with a dry cloth, then spot-clean with a cloth dampened with water and a drop of mild soap. Avoid soaking.
- Drying: air dry flat, away from direct sunlight and heat.
- Storage: roll the mat around a tube or loosely on itself; folding creates permanent creases.
- Avoid: machine washing, bleach, harsh solvents, and ironing unless the confirmed fabric care says otherwise.

Wine and food are common at mahjong tables, so recommend coasters and treating spills before they set.

## Examples

- A customer who spilled red wine should blot first, then spot-clean, and avoid rubbing the stain deeper.
- A customer storing mats between game nights should roll them and keep them out of hot cars.

## Common Mistakes

- Folding mats for storage, which creases the play surface.
- Machine washing before confirming the fabric allows it.
