# Mahjong Mat Collection Overview

## Summary

Bougie Bams mats come in three sizes — 33 inch, 31 inch, and a 20 inch travel size — across designer prints. As of July 2026 the lineup includes the Zsa Zsa Limited Edition, Monroe the Monkey, and Pink Snake Bandana designs.

## Details

Current designs and pricing (verify against the live shop for changes):

- Zsa Zsa Limited Edition: 33 inch at $119, 31 inch at $109
- Monroe the Monkey: 33 inch at $119, 31 inch at $109
- Pink Snake Bandana: 33 inch at $119, 31 inch at $109, 20 inch travel at $65

Pricing pattern: 33 inch mats are $119, 31 inch mats are $109, and travel mats are $65. Limited edition designs may retire without notice.

Mats are the visual anchor of a styled mahjong table and are also given as raffle prizes at Bougie Bams events.

## Examples

- A customer torn between designs can be told the price is the same within a size, so the choice is purely aesthetic.
- A customer asking about a design not listed here should be pointed to the live shop, since the collection rotates.

## Common Mistakes

- Quoting prices without noting they can change; the live shop is authoritative.
- Assuming every design comes in all three sizes — travel size availability varies by design.
