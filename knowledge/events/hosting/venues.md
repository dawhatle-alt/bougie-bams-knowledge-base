# Event Venues

## Summary

Bougie Bams hosts events at intimate north Austin metro venues. Recent venues include Rabbit Hole (24 seats) and Cork2Glass Wine Bar at 601 East Whitestone Blvd in Cedar Park (28 seats).

## Details

Venue profile: the brand favors warm, upscale-casual spaces — wine bars and similar venues — that can seat 24 to 28 guests at tables of four with room for styled tablescapes.

What a venue needs to work: enough four-top tables (or tables that seat four comfortably), lighting that flatters the tables, space for a raffle and welcome area, and drink service that matches the elevated social feel.

Partnership angle: venues gain a sold-out weeknight crowd; Bougie Bams provides all game equipment and styling. Venue inquiries can go to patsy@bougiebams.com.

## Examples

- A guest asking where events happen can be told Cedar Park and nearby north Austin suburbs, with specifics on the events page.
- A wine bar owner interested in hosting should email with seating capacity and available nights.

## Common Mistakes

- Assuming a fixed home venue; locations vary event to event.
- Booking spaces with only large communal tables, which break the four-player format.
