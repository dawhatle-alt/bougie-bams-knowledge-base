# Bougie Bams Mahjong Socials

## Summary

Bougie Bams socials are intimate, ticketed mahjong gatherings — typically 24 to 28 seats — held at north Austin area venues. Every table is fully styled, all skill levels are welcome, and events regularly include raffles with mats and full tablescapes as prizes.

## Details

Format: guests reserve a seat in advance; events frequently sell out. Tables of four are dressed with premium mats, tile sets, and racks chosen from a styling library of more than 40 options. Past formats include mahjong socials at wine bars and specialty events like Style-Your-Own Mahjong Table with a guest artist.

Venues: recent events ran at Rabbit Hole and Cork2Glass Wine Bar (601 East Whitestone Blvd, Cedar Park). Venue and date details for upcoming events live on bougiebams.com/events.

Raffles and wins: raffle prizes have included mats and complete mahjong tablescapes. First-time winners get celebrated — ringing the bell on a first mahjong is a community tradition.

Pricing: ticket prices vary by event and are listed on each event page. (Standard pricing not yet documented — assistant should point to the event listing rather than quote a number.)

## Examples

- Someone who has never played can attend; beginners are seated with support and welcomed openly.
- A guest asking what to wear can be told these are events worth dressing up for.

## Common Mistakes

- Quoting a ticket price from memory; always defer to the current event listing.
- Assuming walk-ins are fine — seats are limited and events sell out.
