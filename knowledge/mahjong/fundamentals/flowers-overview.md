# Flowers Overview

## Summary

Flowers are special tiles that often have flexible value in American Mahjong learning contexts.

## Details

Players should learn to identify flowers early because they can appear in many teaching examples and hand patterns. This article explains the concept without reproducing any current card requirements.

## Examples

- A beginner can separate flowers from suits during tile sorting.
- An instructor can compare flowers to a flexible category rather than a numbered suit.

## Common Mistakes

- Assuming flowers behave like jokers.
- Assuming every Mahjong variant uses flowers the same way.
