# Seating And Table Position

## Summary

Seating establishes turn order and helps players track the direction of play.

## Details

Clear seating habits reduce confusion. Players should know who acts before and after them, where discards belong, and how to keep the table organized.

## Examples

- A host can assign seats before tiles are mixed.
- A beginner can identify the player whose discard they are watching.

## Common Mistakes

- Losing track of whose turn it is.
- Moving tiles or discards into unclear positions.
