# Jokers Overview

## Summary

Jokers are flexible tiles used in certain groupings, with important limits.

## Details

In American Mahjong, jokers can be powerful, but they are not universal substitutes for every situation. Players should learn the broad idea first and consult the current official card or rulebook for official limits.

## Examples

- A joker may help complete a larger matching group in many common teaching scenarios.
- A player should pause before using a joker in a small group or pair.

## Common Mistakes

- Treating a joker as legal everywhere.
- Forgetting that exposed jokers may create exchange opportunities.
