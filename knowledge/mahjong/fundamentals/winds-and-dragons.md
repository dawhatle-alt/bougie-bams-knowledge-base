# Winds And Dragons

## Summary

Winds and dragons are honor tiles that are handled differently from numbered suit tiles.

## Details

These tiles often appear in patterns, calls, and table language. Beginners should learn their names, visual markings, and how they differ from numbered suits before studying strategy.

## Examples

- A player may group all winds together while sorting a starting hand.
- Dragons are often taught as a separate recognition drill.

## Common Mistakes

- Treating honor tiles like numbered suits.
- Forgetting that specific official hand requirements depend on the current card.
