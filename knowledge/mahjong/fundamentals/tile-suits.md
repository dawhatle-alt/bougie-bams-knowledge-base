# Tile Suits

## Summary

The three numbered suits are commonly called Bams, Dots, and Craks.

## Details

Suits help players organize possible patterns. Each suit contains numbered tiles, and players learn to compare suit, number, and grouping when deciding what to keep or discard.

## Examples

- A player may sort all Bams together before comparing possible patterns.
- A teacher can ask beginners to name the suit before naming the number.

## Common Mistakes

- Focusing only on numbers and missing suit differences.
- Using suit nicknames inconsistently at the table.
