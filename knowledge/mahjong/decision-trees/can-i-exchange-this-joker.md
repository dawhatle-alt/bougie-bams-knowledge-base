# Can I Exchange This Joker?

## Summary

Use this decision tree to check whether an exposed joker can be exchanged.

## Details

Decision trees are teaching aids. They do not replace the current official NMJL card or rulebook in formal play.

1. Is the joker visible in another player's exposure?
2. Do you hold the exact natural tile the joker represents?
3. Is it your legal opportunity to make the exchange?
4. If all checks are yes, make the exchange clearly.

## Examples

- Use this at the table before moving tiles.
- Use it in lessons to teach the reasoning sequence.

## Common Mistakes

- Skipping the validity check.
- Using the tree as a substitute for official rules in formal play.
