# Should I Call Or Pass?

## Summary

Use this decision tree to compare progress against information risk.

## Details

Decision trees are teaching aids. They do not replace the current official NMJL card or rulebook in formal play.

1. Will the call complete a group you truly need?
2. Does the exposure keep your hand valid?
3. Does revealing the group help opponents more than it helps you?
4. If the benefit is unclear, passing may preserve flexibility.

## Examples

- Use this at the table before moving tiles.
- Use it in lessons to teach the reasoning sequence.

## Common Mistakes

- Skipping the validity check.
- Using the tree as a substitute for official rules in formal play.
