# Casual Play Etiquette

## Summary

Good etiquette keeps the table friendly, clear, and welcoming.

## Details

Players should announce discards clearly, avoid distracting advice during active decisions, and be patient with learners. The goal is a table where everyone can enjoy the game.

## Examples

- A host can set expectations before play begins.
- A player can ask before giving strategy advice.

## Common Mistakes

- Correcting every move harshly.
- Touching another player's tiles without permission.
