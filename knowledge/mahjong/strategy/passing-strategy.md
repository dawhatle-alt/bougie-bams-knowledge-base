# Passing Strategy

## Summary

Passing strategy helps a player improve their hand while avoiding gifts to opponents.

## Details

During passing phases, players should protect likely useful tiles, pass away low-value extras, and stay alert to duplicate or connected tiles.

## Examples

- A player may keep tiles that support two possible paths.
- A beginner can pass isolated tiles that do not support any plan.

## Common Mistakes

- Passing too quickly.
- Passing tiles that obviously match another player's exposed direction later in the game.
