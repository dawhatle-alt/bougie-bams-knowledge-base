# Choosing A Hand

## Summary

Choosing a hand means selecting a realistic direction based on the tiles you have and the options still available.

## Details

Beginners should look for repeated suits, numbers, honors, flowers, and jokers without forcing one idea too early. In American Mahjong, the current card guides choices; this article does not reproduce card hands.

## Examples

- A player with several related tiles may compare two possible directions before the Charleston ends.
- An instructor can teach players to name a first choice and backup choice.

## Common Mistakes

- Choosing based on one favorite tile.
- Ignoring what has already been discarded.
