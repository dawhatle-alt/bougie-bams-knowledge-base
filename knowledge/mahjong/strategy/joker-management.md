# Joker Management

## Summary

Joker management means using, protecting, and watching jokers with purpose.

## Details

Jokers can strengthen a hand, but they also create visible opportunities when exposed. Players should consider timing, grouping, and exchange risk before revealing them.

## Examples

- A player may delay exposing a joker if it gives opponents an easy exchange.
- A teacher can ask whether the joker is helping a strong plan or hiding uncertainty.

## Common Mistakes

- Spending jokers on a weak direction.
- Forgetting to watch exposed jokers on other racks.
