# Can I Call A Tile For Mahjong?

## Summary

A discarded tile may be callable for Mahjong when it completes a legal winning hand.

## Details

The hand must actually be complete and valid under the current card and rules.

## Examples

- A beginner can ask this question before moving tiles.
- A teacher can answer first, then point to the related rule article.

## Common Mistakes

- Relying on a remembered table habit as official guidance.
- Moving tiles before the table agrees on the answer.
