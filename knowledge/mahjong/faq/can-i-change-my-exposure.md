# Can I Change My Exposure?

## Summary

Once an exposure is made and play has continued, changing it is usually not a casual choice.

## Details

Table timing matters. Stop immediately if a mistake is noticed and consult the applicable rules or host.

## Examples

- A beginner can ask this question before moving tiles.
- A teacher can answer first, then point to the related rule article.

## Common Mistakes

- Relying on a remembered table habit as official guidance.
- Moving tiles before the table agrees on the answer.
