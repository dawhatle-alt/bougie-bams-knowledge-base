# Can I Exchange A Joker?

## Summary

You may exchange only when you have the correct natural tile for an eligible exposed joker.

## Details

Joker exchange depends on the exposure, the matching natural tile, and timing. When in doubt, pause and ask before moving tiles.

## Examples

- A beginner can ask this question before moving tiles.
- A teacher can answer first, then point to the related rule article.

## Common Mistakes

- Relying on a remembered table habit as official guidance.
- Moving tiles before the table agrees on the answer.
