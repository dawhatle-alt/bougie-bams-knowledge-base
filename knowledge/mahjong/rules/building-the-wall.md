# Building The Wall

## Summary

The wall is the arranged supply of face-down tiles used during play.

## Details

Players build the wall after tiles are mixed. The exact table habit may vary, but the goal is a fair, organized supply that supports smooth dealing and drawing.

## Examples

- Each player can build a section of the wall in front of their rack.
- A teacher can focus first on keeping tiles face down and orderly.

## Common Mistakes

- Leaving tiles exposed while building.
- Arguing over house style details before beginners understand the purpose.
