# Turn Sequence

## Summary

A turn normally follows a repeatable rhythm: draw, decide, discard, and allow any permitted reactions.

## Details

Learning the turn sequence gives beginners a mental map for the whole game. Once the rhythm feels natural, special cases such as calls, exposures, and Mahjong declarations are easier to understand.

## Examples

- A teacher can narrate one practice round using the same words each turn.
- A player can wait for the discard before deciding whether a call is possible.

## Common Mistakes

- Drawing before the prior turn is complete.
- Discarding before checking whether the drawn tile changes the plan.
