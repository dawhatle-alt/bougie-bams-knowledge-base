# Joker Exchange

## Summary

A joker exchange lets a player replace an eligible exposed joker with the matching natural tile when the rules allow it.

## Details

Joker exchange is a major part of American Mahjong awareness. Players should recognize exposed jokers, identify the natural tile needed, and follow table timing carefully.

## Examples

- A player notices an exposed group with a joker and holds the natural tile represented by that joker.
- A teacher can frame the exchange as trading the real tile for the flexible tile.

## Common Mistakes

- Trying to exchange from a concealed hand.
- Attempting an exchange without the exact natural tile required.
