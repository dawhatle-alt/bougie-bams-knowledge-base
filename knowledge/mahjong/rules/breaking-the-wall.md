# Breaking The Wall

## Summary

Breaking the wall identifies where dealing begins.

## Details

The wall break is a setup step that creates a starting point for dealing. Players should follow the table's agreed rules and consult the current official materials for formal play.

## Examples

- A host may announce the break clearly so beginners can follow the deal.
- Players can keep hands away until the break is complete.

## Common Mistakes

- Touching tiles before the break is settled.
- Treating a casual shortcut as tournament guidance.
