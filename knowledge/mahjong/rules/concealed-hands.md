# Concealed Hands

## Summary

A concealed hand is played without ordinary exposed calls, subject to the applicable rules.

## Details

Concealed hands require patience because calling may make the hand invalid. This article avoids specific card hands and focuses on the idea that some hand paths restrict exposure.

## Examples

- A player considering a call should first ask whether the target hand must stay concealed.
- An instructor can mark concealed as a planning constraint.

## Common Mistakes

- Calling while pursuing a concealed-only path.
- Assuming concealed means no one can ever declare Mahjong from a discard; official rules should be checked for exact cases.
