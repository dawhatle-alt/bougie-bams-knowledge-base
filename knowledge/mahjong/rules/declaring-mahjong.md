# Declaring Mahjong

## Summary

Declaring Mahjong means announcing that the hand is complete according to the applicable rules.

## Details

Players should pause before declaring, confirm the hand direction, and handle the announcement clearly. Official scoring and hand validity depend on the current card and official rule references.

## Examples

- A player can check tile count and grouping before announcing.
- A teacher can practice the verbal announcement in a sample hand.

## Common Mistakes

- Declaring before verifying the hand.
- Using a casual correction habit in a formal setting.
