# Calling Discarded Tiles

## Summary

Calling is the act of claiming a recently discarded tile when the rules allow it.

## Details

Calls can advance a hand, create an exposure, or complete Mahjong. The player must know whether the desired grouping is eligible before calling. Consult the current official materials for formal edge cases.

## Examples

- A player may call a discard that completes an eligible exposed grouping.
- A teacher can ask what group the call would create before allowing the tile to move.

## Common Mistakes

- Calling because a tile is useful but does not complete a legal call.
- Calling too late after the table has moved on.
