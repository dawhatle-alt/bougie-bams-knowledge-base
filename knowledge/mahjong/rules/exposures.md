# Exposures

## Summary

An exposure is a visible group of tiles placed on the rack after a permitted call.

## Details

Exposures give progress but also reveal information. They can affect joker exchanges, defensive choices, and whether a hand remains concealed.

## Examples

- A called discard may become part of a visible group on the caller's rack.
- Opponents may use an exposure to infer what the player is trying to build.

## Common Mistakes

- Exposing the wrong number of tiles.
- Forgetting that exposed information helps opponents.
