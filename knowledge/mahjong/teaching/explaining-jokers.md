# Explaining Jokers

## Summary

Jokers should be taught as flexible but limited helpers.

## Details

Beginners often overuse jokers because they seem magical. Teach what a joker can generally do, what it cannot always do, and why exposed jokers matter.

## Examples

- Use a practice group to show a joker standing in for a matching tile.
- Then show a separate example where the joker is not appropriate.

## Common Mistakes

- Saying jokers are simply wild for everything.
- Teaching exchange rules before the learner can identify exposed groups.
